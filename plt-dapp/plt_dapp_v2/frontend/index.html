Introduction

PotLockToken is a revolutionary ERC20 token designed to fight the problems plaguing most tokens today — whales controlling liquidity, inactive holders dragging momentum, and no real incentive for community activity. With PotLock, participation isn't optional — it's rewarded. Only one wallet can sell at a time, chosen at random from active holders. That means every holder has a chance to become the seller, creating a fair, rotating incentive model that drives engagement and keeps the ecosystem moving. If you want in on a dynamic, community-powered token, PotLockToken is where you belong.

How it Works

  • Max Token Holdings

    Each wallet can only hold up to 100 tokens, preventing whales from dominating and ensuring broader distribution.

  • Rotating Sell Wallet

    Every 24 hours, one eligible wallet is randomly chosen as the only wallet allowed to sell. This keeps the market orderly and fair.

  • Burn to Rotate

    Once the 24-hour timer expires, anyone can burn tokens to instantly trigger the next wallet rotation — encouraging active participation.

When you buy or swap for PotLockTokens, you are automatically entered in the list of eligible wallets!
Every transaction is subject to a 3% tax defined in the disclaimer at the bottom of the page.
